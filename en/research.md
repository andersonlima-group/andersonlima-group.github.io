## Research directions

### Antibiotic resistance and AmpC β-lactamases
Mechanistic and statistical modeling of antibiotic hydrolysis and inhibition, combining **MD**, **QM/MM**, and **free-energy** approaches.

### Arboviral proteases (NS2B/NS3)
Protein–ligand dynamics, binding energetics, and rational selection of inhibitors using computational workflows.

### Methods
- Molecular dynamics (AMBER)
- QM/MM
- Free energy (US/WHAM)
- ML + interpretability
